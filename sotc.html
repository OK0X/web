Smart OTC

Over-the-counter trading platform is based entirely on Ethereum smart contracts.

What is Smart OTC?

Smart OTC is Over-the-counter based on smart contracts.In this way, all operations are carried out on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring that it cannot be tampered, avoiding human control, and being more secure and trustworthy. This is the main difference between Smart OTC and traditional OTC.

The coins received by the Smart OTC transaction go directly to your own Ethereum wallet, instead of recording the number on a centralized server like an exchange, and you don't actually own your currency.As a result, various security risks such as being attacked, stolen and manually managed are all avoided.

What are the advantages of Smart OTC?

As mentioned above, because it is built on the Ethereum blockchain, it has the following advantages:

  • Highest Security
  • Trusted
  • Permanent
